Minnesota School Employees Association

The project

MSEA came to me with the desire to modernize their outreach in terms of their branding and website. When they told me they had to submit a ticket to someone 3,000 miles away to make a small change to a page, I immediately told them I could help make their lives so much easier with WordPress.

We worked through creating a new logo to go along with their site redesign. And once that was finalized, I worked to give them a site they were proud to show off to current, old, and future members.

My favorite part:

This project was my first freelance project, so the idea of building it on my own was daunting But, once it was finished I felt ready to tackle even more clients. If you want to be one, give me a call!
