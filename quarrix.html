Quarrix

The project

Quarrix is a leader in the specialty roofing products industry and came to High Monkey with a need to redesign their website and re-launch it in Kentico.

They desired a very visual site that would showcase all the different types of products they provide. I designed a way for them to display beautiful images while also using text to take the user to the next page. The variation in content, images, and page types led to a lot of HTML customization while trying to focus on easy editing for page editors.

My favorite part:

Displaying content and images in different orientations
